Welcome to my portifolio !

I am an automation and control engineer, student of data science, in this portfolio are the development of my projects.

Here you will find projects using visualization and data analysis techniques, insights generation, storytelling, machine learning, among others, focused on problem-solving, such as sales forecasting, investment comparison, and applied results.

I started my studies in the area of ​​programming and data science at the end of 2020 after resigning from my job, to focus on my studies, as a pre-sales engineer. I was feeling dissatisfied with the area and what it could bring me so I decided to change, as I always like logic and programming aligned with problem-solving, I looked for related courses, such as introductory DevOps, UX, Design Thinking, and among them the marathon behind the code 2020 from IBM where I had my first contact with data science, immediately arousing my interest, I returned my studies to this beautiful area, entering Alura's data science bootcamp 2020/2021 where I consolidated more knowledge in programming, the vision of business and storytelling, during the Bootcamp I was invited to return to my last company to join the development team. At the beginning of 2021, I joined the DS community, thus continuing my studies in the data area, now reconciling with studies in web development to meet the demands of my new position, but the data area has never lost its place in my heart and objectives, at the end of 2021 I participated in the behind the code marathon edition again, now gaining good positions (among the top 200 LATAM) and being able to deliver projects faster and with higher quality.
